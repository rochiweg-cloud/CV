I am a **Social Data Scientist** working at the intersection of computational methods and social research. Currently, I work as a Data Scientist at the City Government of Buenos Aires, developing machine learning models and integrating multisource data to address urban challenges. Additionally, as a Research Assistant at Factor-Data (UNSAM), I develop computational techniques for social science research.

I am also committed to education in data science, teaching postgraduate courses at the National University of San Martín and professional development courses at [Educación.Earth](https://educacion.earth/). My courses focus on data science applications for social scientists, advanced visualization techniques, and environmental applications of machine learning. My interdisciplinary background allows me to bridge robust social science methodologies with advanced computational techniques.

**Interests**

* Machine Learning
* Spatial Data Analysis
* Mixed Methods Research
* Environmental Data Science
